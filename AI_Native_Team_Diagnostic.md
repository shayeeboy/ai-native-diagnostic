# The AI Native Team Diagnostic

## Why this exists

Most teams are adding AI to a traditional operating model.

Very few are redesigning the operating model itself.

This diagnostic helps you determine:

- Where your team sits today
- What is actually slowing you down
- What needs to change
- What an AI-native team could look like
